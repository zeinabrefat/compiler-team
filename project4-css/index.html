Bangtan Boys (BTS for short) (Hangul: 방탄 소년단; Hanja: 防彈 少年 團) is a seven-member South Korean boy group that was formed in Seoul in 2013. The Seven Co-write and produce many of their songs. The group has organized many tours around the world.
