BANGTAN
The group was formed in adolescence with the release of their debut album “2 Cool for School” (in 2013) under the leadership of the record label Big Hit Entertainment. Their release of several mini albums such as The Most Beautiful Moments in Life, Part 2 (2015), The Best Moments in Life: Young Forever (2016), and The Wings (2016) entered the group for the first time on the Billboard 200 list in the US. In addition to establishing a reputation as a socially conscious group. The album Wings became the band’s first album to achieve sales of one million copies in South Korea alone. By 2017, the band entered the international music market, led the Korean wave to the United States and broke several sales records, becoming the first Korean group to receive certification from the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) for the song "Mic Drop".
