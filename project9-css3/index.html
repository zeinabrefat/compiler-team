The group was formed in adolescence with the release of their debut album “2 Cool for School” (in 2013) under the leadership of the record label Big Hit Entertainment. Their release of several mini albums such as The Most Beautiful Moments in Life, Part 2 (2015), The Best Moments in Life: Young Forever (2016), and The Wings (2016) entered the group for the first time on the Billboard 200 list
